Our Story

From Field Frustration to Field Solution

The Problem We Faced

It started with a $2,500 fine. One of our founders was running a successful lawn care business when an inspector showed up unannounced. The paper logs were in the truck ‚Äî somewhere. By the time they were found, some pages were water-damaged, others illegible.

The fine wasn't just about money. It was the realization that every landscaper faces this same problem: staying compliant while doing the actual work of running a business.

The Search for Solutions

We looked at every option on the market. Enterprise software at $400/month designed for massive ag operations. Clunky apps that took 10 minutes per entry. Spreadsheets that nobody actually used.

Nothing was built for the small crew working job to job, phone in one hand, sprayer in the other.

Building What We Needed

So we built it ourselves. FieldLog Pro started as an internal tool ‚Äî a simple app that let our crews log applications in 30 seconds, right from their phones. Photo capture for labels. Auto-filled weather data. Instant reports.

When other landscapers saw it, they wanted it too. And that's when we realized: this wasn't just our problem to solve. It was everyone's.
